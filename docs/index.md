## ðŸ›  Hardware Options
Popular devices used by our local community.

### **Heltec V3 or V4** (Solid Starter)
**Price:** ~$25 - $35 USD  
The most popular starter board. Affordable, includes a screen, and works great for getting your first node online.  
[View Specs :material-arrow-right:](https://meshtastic.org/docs/hardware/devices/heltec-automation/)

### **RAK WisBlock** (Solar Ready)
**Price:** ~$35 - $50 USD  
Extremely power efficient. Perfect for solar-powered repeaters that you want to leave on a roof or tower.  
[View Specs :material-arrow-right:](https://meshtastic.org/docs/hardware/devices/rak-wireless/)

### **T-Echo** (Portable)
**Price:** ~$50 - $70 USD  
A finished device in a case with an E-Ink screen. Great for carrying in your pocket or bag.  
[View Specs :material-arrow-right:](https://meshtastic.org/docs/hardware/devices/lilygo/)
